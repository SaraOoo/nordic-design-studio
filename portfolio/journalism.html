Journalism & Graphic Design

I have a candidate exam in Journalism from Gothenburg University in Sweden. I’ve worked for 11 years as a Sub Editor for print, a smaller local newspaper and two of Swedens biggest newspaper.

I got into subediting since it was the perfect mix of text and design. Choosing the perfect picture and layout is just as important as making sure the text makes sense and is correct. Writing a punchy and correct headline is an art form!

I started out doing local newspaper pages, moved on to Finance, from there to airy and spacious Culture magazine pages and then into proper News. I did do a quick detour into Sports, but please let's not talk about that.

In 2015 I started filling in as a managing Sub Editor on the night shift at Expressen, and soon after took over that position full time.

Working night shifts at one of Swedens biggest newspaper is not only magical, stressful and fun – it is also teaches you a lot. I am very flexible – want to change something one minute before deadline, sure! – I can handle stress and multiple tasks thrown at me with a breeze.

As a managing Sub Editor I made plans for tonights workflow and the overall rhythm of the paper. I also designed and did the front pages including the many posters all over Sweden.

2018 I turned my old desk job as a subeditor into a freelance gig and I now do the same job, but in front of a beautiful view of the hills in the South of France, and sometimes I write headlines while I dip my feet in the pool.
