Web Developer & Designer

I am a front end developer and web designer. I am currently studying “Breaking Into Tech” at Skillcrush. I have also completed their 10 day BootCamp. I am especially interested in branding, design and Front End Developing, including HTML, CSS and Javascript.

I have always had a interest and love for coding and computers. I learned english when we got our first computer back in the early -90:s. I remember taking some sort of HTML class when internet was really new. Back then it was more about moving flames, little red devils and flashing images than it was about design or function.

In college I studied Graphic Design and that has always followed me hand in hand when I went on to focus on Journalism studies. It became even more prominent when I started working as a sub editor for some of Swedens biggest newspapers.
