Marketing Deck for BrainZone

This was a fun and very quick Design work for a new music business.

The Client is hoping to build an entire new music business and needed a Marketing deck to present to investors and future artists. They were looking for a logo and 10+ pdf pages to present to their first artist. The catch? The deadline was NOW. Having some material to present was more important than having something perfect and completely planned out.

I created a quick logo, a simple color theme and a layout. Two hours later the first draft was in The Clients inbox!
