Marketing Deck for BrainZone

This was a fun and VERY quick design work for a new music business.
The Client is hoping to build an entire new Music Business and needed a Marketing deck to present to investors and future artists.

They were looking for a logo and 10+ pdf pages to present to their first artist.

The catch? The deadline was NOW. Having some sort of material to present was more important than actually having something perfect and completely planned out.


I created a logo, a color theme and a layout. My thought was to make it look serious, but also edgy and with a hint of depth.
Two hours later the first draft was in The Clients inbox!
