about

Hello! And welcome to Nordic Design.studio.

Nordic Design.studio is run by me, Sara Ojasoo.
I help small businesses and influencers stand out with amazing BRANDS and WEBSITES.

I am a Web Designer & Developer with a passion to help small businesses. The one girl show, the girl boss, the influencer, the artist, the blogger, or whatever you want to call yourself.

I create fresh, clean and amazing new brands and websites, or give you that freshen up that will take you above the beginner to a professional level and look.

I have a candidate exam in Journalism and I have worked for over ten years as a Newspaper Designer, Subeditor and managing subeditor at Expressen, one of Swedens biggest newspapers.

I grew up in Sweden on the countryside. On a holiday trip I met the man of my dream, yes really. It turned out that I and this british man had the same dream. When our little son was five months old we relocated to the South of France. The little boy is now two years old and does a lot of running around.

I started Nordic Design.studio 2018 and between trying to be a great mum it is my main passion.
