A Swedish Web Developer & Designer

Nordic Design.studios was started in 2018 by Sara Ojasoo as a way to help growing businesses develop a strong Brand Identity and Online Presence.

Sara is a Visual and Graphic Designer, Web Devloper and UI/UX in the making. But she is also a mum, a journalist, a wife and a pancake lover.

Sara has been working as a subeditor at newspapers for 10 years and 2 years as a managing subeditor.

When she met the man of her dreams she decided it was time to change her life and go chase her dreams. Together they started a family, left their home countries and moved to a mountain side in France.

We are fluent in Swedish and English.
