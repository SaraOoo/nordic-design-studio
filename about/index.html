about

Nordic Design.studio is run by me, Sara Ojasoo.

I help people and small businesses that have outgrown their DIY "okey it will work"-website to create a beautiful and functional website.

Send me an email if you have a project you need help with!

I am a Front End Developer and Web Designer.
I have a background in Journalism with a candidate exam in Journalism. I have worked for over ten years as a Subeditor and Newspaper Designer and Managing Subeditor at Expressen, one of Swedens biggest newspapers. I am also a mum to a little 1,5 year old boy, a wife and a chocolate lover (who isn't?).

I was born in Sweden but when I met the man of my dreams we decided to really go for it and try and live the life of our dreams. One and a half year after we met; our son was born.

In April 2017 we left our home countries and moved to the South of France. As a first time mum, with a little 6 month old boy, moving to a completely new country was overwhelming. House hunting on 3-4 hours of sleep and nappy changing in the middle of nowhere was a experience of a lifetime.

But we did it. We found a house with a garden and a pool and we started working on settling in and building the life we dreamt of.

Working from home with my passion is a luxury – but it is doable! You CAN change your life. You CAN move, quit your job (or change it, I did!) and redo everything.
