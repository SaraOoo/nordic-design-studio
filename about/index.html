A Swedish Web Developer & Designer

Nordic Design.studio is run by me, Sara Ojasoo.
I help growing businesses develop a beautiful and functional websites and a strong brand identity. I can also do paper products, such as business cards or folders. Send me an email if you have a project you need help with!

I am a Front End Developer and web designer in the making. I have a background in journalism and have worked for over ten years as a subeditor (newspaper designer) and managing subeditor at Expressen, one of Swedens biggest newspapers.
I am also a mum, a wife and a pancake lover (mmm, pancakes!).

I was born in Sweden but when I met the man of my dreams we decided to go for our dreams. We started a family, left our home countries and moved to the south of France.
